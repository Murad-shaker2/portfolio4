About the projectl

A multi-purpose social networking platform was developed with the aim of providing an integrated virtual environment for users to communicate and interact seamlessly. The site is distinguished by its ability to enable users to share audio clips, pictures, video clips, links and texts with ease, in addition to the ability to correspond with friends and send messages easily and smoothly. In addition, the site features an easy-to-use store that gives users the opportunity to buy and sell goods and products

Site features:

  • Share audio clips, photos, videos, links and texts easily and seamlessly.
  • The ability to send messages and correspond with friends easily.
  • An easy-to-use store that gives users the opportunity to buy and sell goods and products.
  • The possibility of creating groups for discussion and communication with groups of similar interests.
  • Provide editing tools to customize shared content and make it more attractive.
  • More

Technologies used:

PHP: To program the server part of the website.

JavaScript and jQuery: to achieve interactivity and dynamism in the interface.

HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap: for designing and styling the user interface.

AJAX and JSON: to improve site performance and load data effectively.

PDO and MySQL: to store and manage data securely and efficiently.
