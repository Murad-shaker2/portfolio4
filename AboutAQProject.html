About the projectl

We introduced a new quiz site, where users can enjoy a unique interactive experience. Thanks to our real indicator, participants can determine the correctness of their answers dynamically and instantly, which increases the fun of interacting with the contest. In addition, questions and answers are stored in the browser's local storage, making the user experience extremely fast and convenient.

project features:

  • Various competitions: The site provides a variety of competitions in various fields such as general culture, mathematics, science, and others, which meet the interests of different age groups and interests.
  • Real answer indicator: Users are provided with a real indicator that appears when they choose their answer, as the answer automatically changes color to display whether it is correct or wrong.
  • Simple user interface platform: The website interface is simple and easy to use, making the user experience smooth and enjoyable.
  • Local data storage: Questions and answers are stored in the browser's local storage, allowing for a very fast experience and increasing site responsiveness.

Technologies used:

JavaScript: To achieve interactivity and dynamism in the user interface.
HTML and CSS: to design and format the user interface in an attractive and smooth way.
Local browser storage: To temporarily store questions and answers on the user's device.
